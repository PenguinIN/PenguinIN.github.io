Simulation

To make our test and integration easier we have some pre-configured simulation scenarios that we use to demonstrate the functionalities of our SDK on the Sample App



singlefloor_nav


In this scenario we have the path points starting from O1 floor and ending at the same floor as seen in the following image , you can navigate to 1079 POI to show the error path

Multifloor


In this scenario we have the path points starting from O3 floor to floor O1 as seen in the following images , you can navigate to 1079 POI to show Multi floor Navigation

Multivenue_FM


In this scenario we have the path points starting from FF floor to O3 floor and ending in O1 floor. O3 and O1 floors are in the another venue as seen in the following images , you can navigate to 1079 POI to show Multi Venue Navigation
